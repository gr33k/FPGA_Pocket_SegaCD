# Video and Audio Plan

## Video plan

### Scope (this milestone)
- Keep raw Genesis video path and route to APF video signals.
- No scaling/filtering or video post-processing implementation yet.

### Active mapping
- `video_r/g/b` from internal Genesis source are packed into
  - `video_rgb` (`[23:16]/[15:8]/[7:0]`)
- `video_hs` from internal `video_hsync`
- `video_vs` from internal `video_vsync`
- `video_de` from internal `video_de`
- `video_rgb_clock` from `clk_74a`
- `video_rgb_clock_90` mirrors `video_rgb_clock` in this milestone
- `video_skip` = `0`

### Deferred work
- No scaler tuning and no extra filter chain in this milestone.
- Keep video deterministic for APF probe and smoke validation.

## Audio plan

### Scope (this milestone)
- Keep Genesis `audio_left/audio_right` generated by core wrapper.
- APF output audio transport is stubbed/silent and deterministic.

### Active behavior
- Core emits `core_audio_left/core_audio_right` internally.
- APF-level `audio_dac` is held at a safe silent value.
- `audio_mclk` uses a deterministic local divider stub.
- `audio_adc` is accepted but unused.
- `audio_lrck` is held low while serialization is deferred.

### Deferred work
- No active I2S framing / DAC stream in this milestone.
- No resampling/filters/specialized post-processing.
